Got questions? We have answers

Couldn’t find what you’re after? Drop us a line at earl@s4digi.com and a real human will reply.

What kind of businesses do you work with?

+

EV installers, service providers, suppliers, manufacturers and adjacent energy businesses. If you’re selling installs, hardware or related services in the UK or EU, you’re probably a fit.

Do you offer one-off projects or just monthly packages?

+

Both. Most clients start with a defined project, like a website, a campaign, or a brand refresh, and roll into a monthly retainer once the foundation is in place.

How long does it take to see results?

+

Paid channels can move within the first two weeks. Organic, content and SEO are a 3 to 6 month build. We’re honest about timelines from day one.

Can you work with our existing website or team?

+

Yes. We slot into existing teams as a creative or growth partner, or run things end-to-end. We’re tool-agnostic on stack.

What’s your process like?

+

The S4 Growth System: Strategy, Content, Distribution, Scale. See the section above for the short version, or book an intro call for the long one.
